About ( Pupil Nest ERP Software )

Pupil Nest is a powerful ERP solution created specifically for educational institutions to simplify and optimize school operations. From fees collection with automated reminders and payment tracking to maintaining detailed student profiles and managing academic assessments, Pupil Nest ensures that all essential data is organized, accessible, and easy to manage. The platform also streamlines staff management by tracking qualifications, roles, leave requests, and payroll processes, enabling efficient administration.

With robust attendance tracking, automated reporting, and a comprehensive view of school operations, Pupil Nest equips institutions with the insights needed for informed decision-making. This all-in-one solution supports smoother communication and enhanced learning outcomes, making it the ideal choice for schools seeking to modernize and elevate their administrative processes.
